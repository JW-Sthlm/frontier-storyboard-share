Why this exists

Partners need a practical path from AI curiosity to AI-first delivery.

Partner delivery is changing fast. Agents and MCP-connected tools are starting to handle the parts of delivery that used to eat consultant hours: backlog grooming, scaffolding, infrastructure, test, the admin around every project. The business case is already solid. Customer expectations will follow. The partners building this motion now have a real window to differentiate, and in a year or two, AI-first delivery becomes the baseline.

Frontier Consultancy is the motion that helps partners get there. Today it runs across two tracks: Cloud and AI Platform, and AI Business Solutions for Dynamics partners. More in the pipeline. You walk through what AI-first delivery looks like in your space, the delivery paths your team can adopt, and hands-on exercises where you design reusable agents you can put to work on projects you're already running.

Everyone leaves with a curated set of skills, agents, and MCP servers ready to install in your own toolstack like Copilot CLI, VS Code, whatever you use, so the change doesn't end when the workshop does. The intent is simple. Give you the knowledge and the tools to start delivering this way, on real projects, the week after.
